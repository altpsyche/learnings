> A graphics programmer's path through modern AI, taught through real-time visuals, learned deeply enough to teach, then shipped as blog, YouTube, and altpsyche.dev shaders.

## Goal
Learn the full path from arithmetic to the 2026 frontier well enough to teach it, and produce a 33-episode series narrating each from-scratch build three ways: code, visual, and written or spoken.

## Plan documents
- [[series-bible]] (the full curriculum: 8 modules, 33 episodes, the through-line, the visuals)
- [[enrollment-plan]] (existing courses mapped bottom to top onto the bible)

## How this maps into the vault
Full rules: [[workflows]]. In short:
- Each episode is a folder, episodes/epNN-slug/, with a dashboard hub (t-episode) plus epNN-script.md and epNN-blog.md.
- Each concept is an atomic note in Notes, written as you learn it, shared with moc-graphics and moc-math where it overlaps.
- Each paper, course, or book is a source note in Sources.
- Real code, shader, blog, and video host elsewhere. The vault drafts and plans them and tracks links and per-channel status in the hub. Board: Maps/episodes.base.
- Concepts are born when learned, not pre-stubbed. The bible stays the master index.
