## Summary
The cleanest single source for the math floor of ML. Chapters 2 to 4 cover linear algebra, analytic geometry, and matrix decompositions, which are the spine of [[ep01-linear-algebra]]. Free PDF online.

## Key claims
- Linear algebra reframed around transformations of space rather than grids of numbers.
- Inner products give length, angle, and projection (see [[dot-product]]).
- Eigendecomposition and what it reveals about a transform (see [[eigenvector]]).
